Introduction

  • Setup, obtain mlnscript from the Centre of Multilevel Modelling.

  • Investigation of the binary and library to check it’s a universal binary

    % lipo -archs /Applications/MLwiN/mlnscript
x86_64 arm64
% lipo -archs /Applications/MLwiN/libmln.dylib
x86_64 arm64

  • Install R2MLwiN (Zhang et al. 2016) from CRAN.

    install.packages("R2MLwiN")

Run an example

library(R2MLwiN)
options(MLwiN_path = "/Applications/MLwiN/mlnscript")
